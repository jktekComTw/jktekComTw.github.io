About JiaoKun Technology (昭群科技)

Our Story

JiaoKun Technology, established in Taiwan, represents the convergence of innovation and engineering excellence in the heart of Kaohsiung. Founded and led by Carlos Ma (馬嘉鴻), our company has positioned itself as a trusted partner for businesses seeking cutting-edge technological solutions across multiple domains.

Located in Fengshan District, Kaohsiung City, we serve as a bridge between traditional engineering practices and modern technological demands, delivering comprehensive solutions that drive industrial advancement and digital transformation.

Our Mission

At JiaoKun Technology, we are committed to empowering businesses through innovative technology solutions. Our mission is to provide world-class embedded systems, power electronics, and digital solutions that enhance operational efficiency, reduce costs, and accelerate time-to-market for our clients' products and services.

Core Expertise

Embedded Systems Development

Our team specializes in GNU/Linux and RTOS embedded system development, creating robust, scalable solutions for industrial applications. We leverage open-source technologies to deliver cost-effective, customizable systems that meet the most demanding performance requirements.

Motor Control & Power Electronics

We excel in AC motor FOC (Field-Oriented Control) development and motor inverter circuit design. Our expertise in power electronics enables us to create efficient, reliable motor control systems for industrial automation, renewable energy, and transportation applications.

Digital Innovation

Our comprehensive power electronics product design services cover everything from concept to production, ensuring optimal performance and reliability. We combine theoretical knowledge with practical engineering experience to deliver solutions that exceed industry standards.

Software Development

We provide end-to-end web development and mobile APP development services, creating user-friendly interfaces and robust backend systems. Our development approach focuses on scalability, security, and user experience.

Engineering Design

Our mechanism 3D design capabilities enable us to transform concepts into tangible products. We utilize advanced CAD tools and simulation software to optimize designs for manufacturability and performance.

Digital Circuit Design

We offer specialized digital IC design services, developing custom integrated circuits that meet specific application requirements while optimizing for power consumption and performance.
