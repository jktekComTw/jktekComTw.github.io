## Daily Study Routine Recommendation

### Study Session Structure (2-3 hours daily):
1. **Review (15 min):** Previous day's material and key concepts
2. **Reading (60-90 min):** New sections with note-taking
3. **Problem Solving (60-90 min):** Work through assigned exercises
4. **Summary (15 min):** Write key takeaways and questions

### Weekly Activities:
- **Day 7 of each week:** Full chapter review and additional problems
- **End of Week 4:** Midterm self-assessment (Chapters 1-5)
- **End of Week 8:** Final comprehensive review

### Problem-Solving Strategy:
1. Read problem carefully and identify given information
2. Draw diagrams where applicable
3. List relevant equations and concepts
4. Solve systematically, showing all steps
5. Check units and reasonableness of answer
6. Review solution method
