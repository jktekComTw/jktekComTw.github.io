Convention notes:
  • Brown & Vranesic (this textbook) uses · for AND and + for OR, mirroring algebraic notation.
  • Computer-science / propositional-logic texts prefer ∧ / ∨ / ¬.
  • Programming-language operators & / | / ^ perform the same operations bit-by-bit.
  • Both NAND (↑) and NOR (↓) are functionally complete — any Boolean function can be built from either alone.
